The Miracle of Love Trilogy

It took me about a year to write this, and I originally had no intention of sharing it. I think its my first piece of poetry that I've felt compelled to include here. The title was inspired by a song titled "Miracle of Love" by Swans, although their commonalities stop there as they are quite different. The only thing they share is being about love. Thats about all I have to say about it, I'll let its contents speak for itself.
