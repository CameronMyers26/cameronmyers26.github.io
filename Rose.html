Time hangs in suspension
While our hearts connect
And faith in that bond ensures
Even in a world of ruin

And space waits in abeyance
Until moments burn into monuments
Every struggle is just a hiccup
In the face of forever and always
Unconditional love across vast distance

I have found mine

The veil of delirium falls gracefully
